\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Applied Research (Internal)\
\
This folder contains **internal research and working documents**\
created while contributing to the preparation of an AI module\
for a Lean Yellow Belt training.\
\
The materials were developed in a collaborative and exploratory context\
and were **not intended as public-facing or polished deliverables**.\
\
---\
\
## Included\
\
### Cross-Sector AI Research\
\
Exploratory research across multiple sectors to identify:\
- common patterns in AI adoption\
- opportunities to explain AI concepts within a Lean and process-improvement context\
- supporting insights for training content development\
\
The focus was on synthesis, structuring information,\
and supporting early-stage decision-making.\
